About Mystery Night Three

“There is not a lot we can do to help you in your cause to find the keycode to the vault. However, there is one thing we can do!”

-Mystery Night Three

Mystery Night Three was the groundbreaking & dynamic new Mystery Night, redefining how the Nights are made.

“Contact notareal@emailaddress.com to cancel your Untitled Magazine subscription...”

-Tom Campbell, Mystery Night Three

Mystery Night Three switches the roles of good and evil. No more is the crime solving, now we're committing a robbery! The goal of MN3 is to break into the Hawthorn Bank and steal from their "impenetrable" vault said to be full of riches beyond imagination. Whilst it's a bit lighter on story this time around, Mystery Night Three will leave you shocked with its dynamic, open-ended problem solving. No more is the linear puzzle-after-puzzle system of Mystery Nights, this time you're put in a room and you're on your own. Search for clues, complete puzzles, and piece the story together. And once you open the box of secrets in the room, you will be privy to yet more clues that will eventually reveal the secret code to the vault of Hawthorn Bank. Good luck!

“Remember, get out of the building by 8:40. If you stay in the building longer than that, you will be captured and killed.”

-Mystery Night Three
