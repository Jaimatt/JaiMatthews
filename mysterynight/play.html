Play Mystery Night One

Mystery Night One is available to play in full using the online simulation below. Some pages will be videos, whilst others will be puzzles. You must make sure you watch the videos when they come up or you will not understand the plot. When you arrive at a puzzle, ensure you understand your solution and do not solve it using trial and error. Hints are available. This program runs with JavaScript and is, therefore, not impervious to cheating. However, if you do cheat, you will not have fun. So, don't. Enjoy!
