About Mystery Night

The Mystery Nights are a series of puzzle-adventures created by Jai Matthews & Aidan de Sousa. Each has an overarching story that we have created and goes through a series of original puzzles and logical challenges. Initially Mystery Night was created for an event at Hawthorn Scouts, though these days it exists outside of these confines. At present, there have been a total of 4 Mystery Nights that released from 2019-2022, the last of which was finished in September 2022.
