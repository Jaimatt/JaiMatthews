About Mystery Night Two

Authors: Jai Matthews
Aidan de Sousa
Date Created: March 2020
Estimated Runtime: 60-80 mins
Difficulty: Medium


“If the curator finds out this happened I’ll be arrested immediately. You've only got until 8:45 before the curator arrives for work.”

-Danny, Mystery Night Two

With a wealth of characters and split into 7 parts, Mystery Night two was the epic sequel to the original.

“All I remember was that there was a diamond to the left of a spade and that there was an ace to the left of a heart.”

-Lady Shaw, Mystery Night Two

In Mystery Night two, the famous "Starry Night" painting by Vincent Van Gogh has gone missing! And, of course, it's up to you to find it and bring it back to the Museum of Modern Art. Mystery Night two is similar to the first one structurally, but also manages to expand upon the gameplay. This time split into 7 parts, Mystery Night two has a much more story-based gameplay. You will meet many characters, travel all across New York, and hunt down the thieves that have taken the painting. And Mystery Night Two still has the puzzles you know and love from Mystery Night One, this time with motifs and references to Doctor Who. The epic sequel to Mystery Night has a fun narrative, some epic puzzles, and a shock ending that will leave your jaw on the floor.

“But just as you’re ready to give up, you notice a piece of paper sitting on the table.”

-Mystery Night Two
