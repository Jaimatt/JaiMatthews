Mystery Night Four saw the series reach new ground... literally.

“One of these divisions is thought to have ascended into the mountains, hence evolving into adequate mountaineers”

-Starship Darlington, Mystery Night Four

The fourth Mystery Night, Starship Darlington, takes to the skies in a new installment. This time, set in space. But wait- Cue the flashing lights, the alarms, the panic. The spaceship is crashing! In 90 minutes, the Starship Darlington will collide on the surface of an uninhabited planet and it's up to YOU to save everyone. Technical officer stevenson is calling you and needs your help. Help him crack codes, find keys, and hack systems. For this first time in Mystery Night history, this installment features software made exclusively for MN4. Countless new puzzle-solving possibilities are opened by the presence of a laptop/ control-panel in the room of Mystery Night Four. So prepare yourself for the new sci-fi themed adventure in this latest Mystery Night. Enjoy!
