About Mystery Night Five

Authors: Jai Matthews
Aidan de Sousa
Date Created: September 2023
Estimated Runtime: 60 mins
Difficulty: Medium


“All right; this situation is much worse than I thought. I’d better go and reassess my own security.”

-Harper, Mystery Night Five

Mystery Night Five took the technology used in MN4, pushed it even further, and made it even better!

“It wasn’t always like this. Victor used to be kind. We would talk and the hours would fly by. He was a true friend. And there was Elliot too. Victor, Ava, and Elliot; an inseparable trio. The V-E-A. But then, there was the accident.”

-Ava, Mystery Night Five

In Mystery Night Five we’ve brought back everything we loved about MN4: the dynamic digital interface & the voice acted transmissions. And this time it follows a new narrative about the tragic story of three friends, and the lengths they’ll go to for each other. So, what was the big thing we changed in Mystery Night Five? The puzzles! Mystery Night Four was regrettably not very innovative in this domain so we’ve done away with our go-to puzzles and designed some new systems. The first third consists of one giant map that interconnects several puzzles organically, asking people to consider the whole thing at once. The second third greatly utilises the digital interface, and the final third of the mystery night consists of the puzzle design you know and love Mystery Nights 1-4, with their difficulties dialled right up!

“The time has come. I will now activate the machine!”

-Victor, Mystery Night Five
