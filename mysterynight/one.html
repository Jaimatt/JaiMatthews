About Mystery Night

Authors: Jai Matthews

Mitchell Abberdan
Date Created: June 2019
Estimated Runtime: 60 mins
Difficulty: Medium


“This message was intended for an elite group of specialised detectives. If this description does not fit you, then you will not be allowed to access this information.”

-Michael, Mystery Night

Mysetry Night was the first that kickstarted a series of puzzle adventures by Jai Matthews & Aidan de Sousa.

“I will forever be in your debt if you can help me, thank you in advance.”

-Michael, Mystery Night

The first Mystery Night follows a very simple premise. Jennifer Wilson is dead and YOU need to find out who killed her. With characters from and allusions to Conan Doyle's famous Sherlock Holmes stories, Mystery Night One follows a classic murder mystery narrative. Players are drawn through a world of puzzles and challenges and are aided by people such as their friend, Michael, or their cruel boss, Alistair. The story is split into two parts. During the first of these you move through 5 unique, custom-made logical challenges by Jai Matthews & Aidan de Sousa to find out who killed Jennifer. In the second part of the story you are greeted with 5 new puzzles in an attempt to determine where the killer is.

“If you aren’t aware, Jennifer Wilson was murdered. I will not allow anyone here to investigate this crime. This is not to be dealt with by you.”

-Alistair, Mystery Night
